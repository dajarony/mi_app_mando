# Privacy Policy for Smart TV Manager

**Last Updated: December 15, 2024**

## Introduction

Smart TV Manager ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **TV Device Information**: IP addresses and device names of Smart TVs you register in the app
- **App Preferences**: Your theme settings and app configuration

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Local Network Data**: The app scans your local network to discover Smart TVs. This information is stored locally on your device only.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:
- Enable you to control your Smart TVs
- Save your preferences and registered devices
- Improve app functionality

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We do not:
- Collect personal identification information
- Store data on external servers
- Share your information with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services

## Network Access

The app requires internet permission to:
- Communicate with Smart TVs on your local network
- Send control commands (volume, channels, power, etc.)

**The app only communicates within your local network and does not send data to external servers.**

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
